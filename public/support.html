Need a hand with Pippin? We are happy to help.

Contact us

Email info@underbridges.co.za and we will get back to you. Tell us what happened and, if you can, which device you are using.

Common questions

  • I forgot my password. On the log in screen, tap "Forgot password" and we will email you a reset link.
  • The meal scanner could not read my photo. The estimate is AI based and not always perfect. Try again with more light, or log the meal manually. Estimates are guidance, not medical advice.
  • How do I delete my account? Open Settings and tap "Delete account". This permanently removes your profile, logged data, posts, and photos.
  • How do I add friends? Open the Squad tab and search for a friend, or share your invite link.

Health note

Pippin is a health and wellness tracker, not a medical device or a source of medical advice. Always talk to a qualified professional about your health.
