Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

This policy explains what information Pippin collects, why, and the choices you have. We keep it plain and short on purpose.

Pippin is a health and wellness tracker, not a medical device or a source of medical advice. Nothing in the app is a diagnosis or treatment. Always talk to a qualified professional about your health.

Who we are

Pippin ("we", "us") is operated from South Africa and available at pippin.co.za. For anything in this policy, contact us at privacy@pippin.co.za. We process personal information in line with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

What we collect

  • Account details you give us: your name, email address, and password. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email, and profile picture from Google instead of a password.
  • Health and activity data you log: meals and meal photos, calories, steps, workouts, water, sleep, moods, goals, and streaks.
  • Community content you create: posts, photos, reactions, comments, friends, and circles.
  • Usage data: XP, badges, level, and basic technical information your browser sends (such as device type) needed to run and secure the service.

How we use it

  • To run your account, save your progress, and show your stats and mascot.
  • To analyse a meal photo you snap and estimate its calories (see "Meal photos" below).
  • To power the social features you opt into: feeds, friends, cheers, and comments.
  • To send you essential emails (welcome, password resets, account notices).
  • To keep the service secure and prevent abuse.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

Meal photos and AI

When you snap a meal, the image is sent to Google's Gemini model to estimate the food and its calories. When you share a photo to the community, it is stored in a publicly accessible location so it can appear in the feed, meaning anyone with the link could view it. Please do not share photos that contain sensitive or identifying information you would not want to be public.

Who we share it with

We use a small number of trusted service providers ("operators" under POPIA) purely to run Pippin. They process data on our instructions:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and file storage.
  • Vercel — website hosting and delivery.
  • Google — optional sign-in, and Gemini for meal photo analysis.
  • Resend — sending our transactional emails.

Some of these providers process data outside South Africa. We rely on their security commitments to keep your data protected in transit and at rest. We may also disclose information if the law requires it.

How long we keep it

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data within a reasonable period, except where we must keep something to meet a legal obligation.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your personal information, or to stop certain processing. Email privacy@pippin.co.za and we will help. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.

Children

Pippin is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has given us data, contact us and we will remove it.

Storage on your device

Pippin stores data in your browser (local storage) to keep you signed in and to run the app. Clearing your browser storage will sign you out. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

Changes

If we make material changes to this policy we will update the date above and, where appropriate, let you know in the app.

Questions? We would genuinely like to hear them: privacy@pippin.co.za.
